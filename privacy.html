Privacy Protocol

Document ID: TRC-PRIV-2026-A

1. Geospatial Data (GPS)

Tactical Range Card PRO utilizes high-precision GNSS sensors to calculate Earth-rotation (Coriolis) and target vector solutions. This data is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit your live location to external servers unless you explicitly engage the 'Tac Comms' mission mode, which shares location only with your authenticated team members.

2. Optical & Acoustic Sensors

Access to your device's Camera and Microphone is required for situational awareness features (Body Cam/Surveillance Hub) and PTT voice communications. All video and audio data is processed end-to-end between your tactical units. We do not record or store your surveillance feeds on our infrastructure.

3. Local Storage & IndexedDB

To ensure total offline reliability (Airplane Mode), all range cards, profiles, and intel snapshots are stored in an encrypted local database (IndexedDB) on your device. This data remains on your hardware until you manually delete it or clear the application cache.
